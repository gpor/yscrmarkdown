Google Cloud.

Home / Solutions

Managed services for Google Cloud Platform.

Bright Labs builds, tests, and deploys your websites and digital applications on the Google Cloud Platform offering a secure, scalable, and completely managed, infrastructure solution.

Google Cloud Platform (GCP), offered by Google, is a suite of cloud computing services that runs on the same infrastructure that Google uses internally for its end-user products, such as Google Search and YouTube. Named a 'Leader' by Gartner in its Magic Quadrant for Cloud Infrastructure as a Service (2019), GCP provides a series of modular cloud services including computing, data storage, data analytics and machine learning.

Want to host on a fast, reliable network?

Give your customers the same experience you get every time you go to Google: a website that loads quickly and doesn't crash. With an expansive global network, your website can handle sudden spikes in traffic without missing a beat.

Protect your site, protect your customers

Rely on GCP's multilayered secured infrastructure, regular patches with no planned outages, and innovative technology like Global Load Balancing for protection against hackers and Denial of Service attacks.
