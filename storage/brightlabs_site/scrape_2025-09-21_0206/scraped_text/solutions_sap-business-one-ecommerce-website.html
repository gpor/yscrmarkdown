SAP E-Commerce solutions for digital transformation.

Our SAP integration services allow you to extend your SAP instance to the digital world linking the data and processes to E-Commerce stores, websites, portals and online applications.

SAP is ERP software used by over 40,000 small to medium-sized businesses worldwide. The software connects and streamlines businesses processes - and grows along with you. SAP B1 is used across consumer product industries, manufacturing, professional services, retail, and wholesale distribution.

Focusing on growth facilitation, SAP allows companies to streamline their processes and introduce automation to stay productive and profitable. Core functions include financial management, sales and customer management, purchasing and inventory control, business intelligence, and analytics with reporting.

Our SAP integration services

  • Transfer online sales orders into your SAP Business One system
  • Synchronise product data with your E-Commerce store
  • Synchronise customer data between SAP Business One, your E-Commerce store, CRM and other applications
  • Develop personalised communication for your customer base
  • Automate business processes unique to your business on a customisation basis
